Solar System Installation Process

Evaluation & Consultation

Begin by assessing the feasibility of solar installation for the property. Evaluate factors such as roof orientation, available space, and energy requirements through a comprehensive consultation process.

Custom Design & Proposal

Develop a personalized solar system design that aligns with the homeowner's needs and preferences. Present a detailed proposal outlining the system specifications, estimated energy production, financial investment, and potential returns.

Financial Planning & Permitting

Assist the homeowner in exploring financing options and incentives available for solar installation. Guide them through the process of obtaining necessary permits and approvals from local authorities to ensure compliance with regulations.

Professional Installation

Execute the installation process with precision and expertise. Securely mount solar panels on the designated area, install inverters, and meticulously connect all components to create a seamless solar energy system.

Thorough Inspection & Activation

Ensure system integrity and proper functionality through comprehensive inspections. Test the performance of solar panels and inverters. Once approved, activate the system and ensure seamless operation.
